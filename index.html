Welcome to KETNYO Herbal Clinic

Cancer & Tumor

Cancer is a disease in which cells, almost anywhere in the body, begin to divide uncontrollably. A tumor is when this uncontrolled growth occurs in solid tissue such as an organ, muscle, or bone.

Chronic wounds

A chronic wound is a wound that does not heal in an orderly set of stages and in a predictable amount of time the way most wounds do; wounds that do not heal within three months are often considered chronic.

Ulcers

A sore that develops on the lining of the oesophagus, stomach or small intestine.

Arthritis

Inflammation of one or more joints, causing pain and stiffness that can worsen with age.

Fibroids

Non-cancerous growths in the uterus that can develop during a woman's childbearing years.
